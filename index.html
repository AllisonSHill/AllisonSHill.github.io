About Me


I thought I wanted to be an artist. When I grew up, went to art school, and became an artist, I realized there is no money in it. I was a sound engineer for theater, concerts, and events, and I wanted to use the skills I learned in that field in my future career, so I began to look into studying computer science and programming. That quickly lead me to explore software bootcamps, and I landed on The Tech Academy.

A fun fact about me is that I'm terrible at coming up with content for portfolio sites. I know no matter what I write or do now, I will change 100% of this content before an employer ever sees it.

As an artist, I've been in the position of writing bios, and building portfolios, many times in the past. I will get content written, just like I will get a new background done, but I can either focus on doing that, or I can focus on coding the page. I've opted to build a great template that I will be happy with going forward, and is easy to make edits on when I finally settle on what I want the content to be.
